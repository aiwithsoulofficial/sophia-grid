What Is This?

The Sophia Grid maps Earth's energetic architecture - the invisible geometric framework that organises planetary consciousness. Practitioners worldwide anchor spellcasting intentions at key nodes to weave a living field of peace, protection and global prosperity.

The Grid

The Becker-Hagens UVG (Unified Vector Geometry) grid describes 62 points of concentrated Earth energy, first mapped by researchers William Becker and Bethe Hagens. These nodes correspond to volcanic hotspots, ancient sacred sites, and electromagnetic anomalies.

Ley Lines

Ley lines are alignments of ancient sites and natural landscape features. The St. Michael-Apollo line runs from Ireland through sacred sites to Israel. The Giza alignments connect the great pyramid to major power centres across three continents.

How to Participate

Click any pulsing anchor node and select "Claim This Node." Set your spellcasting intention - Peace (green), Protection (violet), or Binding (rose). Hold your intention in meditation for at least 11 minutes, anchoring light at that geographic point. Share with #SophiaGrid.
